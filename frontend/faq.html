FAQ

  • Q: Does Sovren need the internet?
    A: No, it works 100% offline.
  • Q: Is my data stored online?
    A: Never. All data stays on your device.
  • Q: What kind of PDFs can I upload?
    A: Any academic, legal, or institutional PDF under ~50MB.
  • Q: Can it run on low-end laptops?
    A: Yes, with at least 4GB RAM and no GPU required.
